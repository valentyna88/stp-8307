How to play

  • Deploy & Engage - Enter combat zones and eliminate enemy threats.
  • Select Your Arsenal - Equip powerful rifles, grenades, and tactical gear.
  • Complete Missions - Execute stealth takedowns, rescues, and battlefield assaults.
  • Coordinate with Allies - Lead your team in co-op or multiplayer missions.
  • Upgrade & Customize - Enhance your weapons, armor, and combat skills.
  • Rise in the Ranks - Dominate leaderboards in competitive PvP battles.
  • Unlock Special Ops Challenges - Test your skills in elite missions for rare rewards.
