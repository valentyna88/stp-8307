Main Gameplay Features

  • Realistic Combat Missions - Engage in high-intensity battles across various war zones.

  • Advanced Weaponry Unlock and upgrade authentic military-grade firearms and gear.

  • Diverse Battle Environments - Fight through urban warfare, jungle operations, and desert conflicts.

  • PvE & PvP Modes - Engage in both story-driven campaigns and competitive multiplayer battles.

  • Stealth & Assault Options - Choose your playstyle: silent assassin or full-force assault.

  • Special Operations Challenges - Complete classified missions for exclusive rewards
